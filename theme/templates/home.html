Welcome to IntelliSched

IntelliSched is an AI-assisted scheduling platform that simplifies instructor-subject matching and automates schedule generation for colleges and universities. Whether you're a Department Head, or Instructor — everything you need is right here.

AI Assisted Matching

Leverage pretrained AI models to semantically match instructors to subjects by analyzing their preferences, qualifications, and teaching history—ensuring more relevant and effective assignments.

Smart Scheduling

Generate conflict-free, load-balanced schedules using Google OR-Tools. Fully managed by the department head.

Instructor Tools

Manage availability, teaching preferences, credentials, and historical teaching data in one place.

Real-time Audit Logs

Track scheduling changes, account updates, and assignment adjustments for accountability and transparency.

Role-based Dashboards

Custom dashboards for each role: department head and instructors — optimized for workflow.
